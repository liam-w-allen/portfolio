For the last three years I've attended the Mayors Summer Employment Program (MYSEP), and learned some valuable lessons like the importance of knowing your worth, specificities on how to carry myself in a professional environment, and the weight held by punctuality.

MYSEP Y1

In my first year with MYSEP, I participated in making a plan with the city to create a floating wetland in the Charles River. It contained canvassing residents in the Kendall area about what they think about the project, and then drawing up plans based on feedback.

MYSEP Y2

The second time around, I was with The Williams Agency, a coalition of local businesses who promote sustainability, community, and equity. I worked with them in data entry, property restoration, and event planning.

MYSEP Y3

My final year in the program consisted of learning and teaching parkour at Parkour Generations Boston, a local chapter in a countrywide organization who's goal is to bring the art of parkour to the masses and lower the barrier to entry.
